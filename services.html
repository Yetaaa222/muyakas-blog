What I Offer

Professional Services Designed for Impact

Each service is delivered with rigorous methodology, deep sector knowledge, and a genuine commitment to lasting organisational improvement.

Governance, Risk & Compliance Advisory

End-to-end GRC programme design and implementation. We help organisations build governance frameworks, risk registers, and compliance architectures that are practical, robust, and aligned with international standards including ISO 37000, King IV, and COSO ERM.

Governance framework design Risk appetite definition Regulatory compliance mapping Policy architecture

Mentorship & Coaching

One-on-one and group mentorship for compliance professionals, emerging leaders, and executives navigating complex regulatory environments. Programmes are tailored to individual career trajectories and organisational contexts.

Executive coaching Professional GRC mentorship Leadership development Career pathway planning

People & Organisations Development

Building resilient, people-centred organisations through structural design, culture change, and capability building. We align human resources strategy with governance objectives to create organisations that perform sustainably.

Organisational design Culture change management HR governance frameworks Capacity building programmes

Development of SOPs

Writing and implementing Standard Operating Procedures that are clear, contextual, and genuinely adopted. We specialise in SOPs for finance, HR, procurement, grants management, and compliance functions across NGO and corporate settings.

Finance & procurement SOPs HR & people operations SOPs Compliance procedure manuals SOP training & rollout

Assets Management

Designing and implementing asset management frameworks that ensure accountability, optimal utilisation, and regulatory compliance. Covering fixed assets, IT assets, and programmatic assets across complex multi-site organisations.

Asset register development Lifecycle management policy Asset verification systems Disposal frameworks

Financial Management & Grants Oversight

Supporting organisations with robust financial governance, donor reporting, and grants management controls. We help NGOs and public benefit organisations meet USAID, EU, UN, and other institutional donor requirements.

Grants management systems Donor compliance frameworks Financial oversight policies Sub-grantee management
How I Work

01

Discovery
